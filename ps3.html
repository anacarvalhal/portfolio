Overview

><Command and Control for air operations

PS3 (Portuguese Sky Sentinel System) is a Command and Control (C2) platform developed for the Portuguese Air Force to support the planning, monitoring, and execution of operational missions. Initially created to support the Special Rural Fire Fighting Device (DECIR), the platform later evolved to address Search and Rescue (SAR) requirements, enabling the management of the full incident lifecycle in accordance with IAMSAR regulations.

The system provides mission planning and monitoring capabilities, bidirectional communication with aircraft, and interoperability with both Portuguese Air Force systems and external military and civilian stakeholders.

PS3 was built directly upon Oversee, the command and control platform originally designed and developed for the Portuguese Navy. Rather than starting from scratch, the redesign took the Navy's proven C2 foundation — its core concepts, workflows and interaction patterns — and adapted it to the specific needs of air operations, carrying over what already worked while reshaping it for the Air Force context.
